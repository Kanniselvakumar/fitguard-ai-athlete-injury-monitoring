# FitGuard – AI-Based Athlete Health & Injury Risk Monitoring System

FitGuard is an AI-powered sports analytics platform that monitors athlete training activity, tracks recovery metrics, and predicts injury risk using machine learning. The system combines sports science metrics, health tracking, and intelligent recommendations to help athletes train smarter and reduce the risk of overtraining injuries.

---

## 🚀 Features

### Athlete Training Monitoring
- Training session logging (duration, intensity, distance)
- Weekly and monthly training load tracking
- Heart rate activity monitoring
- Distance and duration analytics

### Analytics & Visualization
- Training load trends (weekly/monthly line charts)
- Fatigue level gauge visualization
- Injury risk progression over time
- Heart rate zone breakdown charts
- Distance and duration heatmap calendar
- Recovery vs training balance chart

### 🧠 Machine Learning & AI
- Random Forest model for injury risk prediction
- Feature importance visualization
- Prediction confidence analysis
- Model performance metrics (precision, recall, F1-score)
- Retrain model when new athlete data accumulates
- AI Smart Coach for personalized training recommendations

### 💤 Recovery & Wellness Tracking
- Sleep hours logging
- Rest day tracking
- Recovery score trends
- Hydration tracking
- Overtraining alert system

### 🔔 Alerts & Notifications
- High injury risk alerts
- Training inactivity reminders
- Weekly performance summaries
- Pre-session fatigue warnings

### 👤 Athlete Profile
- Athlete profile management
- Sport-specific settings
- Injury history tracking
- Personal records monitoring

### 📅 Training Planning
- Weekly training calendar
- Goal setting (distance, sessions per week)
- Recommended rest days
- AI-generated weekly training plans

### 📈 Advanced Sports Analytics
- Acute:Chronic Workload Ratio (ACWR)
- Training monotony score
- Training strain score
- Risk vs performance correlation analysis
